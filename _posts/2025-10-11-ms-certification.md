If you are preparing for a Microsoft Certification exam, you may be interested in newest free exam preparation resource [Practice Assessments for Microsoft Certifications](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/practice-assessments-for-microsoft-certifications). It allows you to assess your knowledge so that you can be better prepared for exam. These questions are not the same as you will see on the exam, but they are examples only to provide insight what to expect.
