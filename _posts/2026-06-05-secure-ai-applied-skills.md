I'd like to share another achievement – I passed the [Microsoft Applied Skills: Secure AI solutions in the cloud](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/applied-skills/secure-ai-solutions-in-the-cloud/) assessment, confirmed by [my credential](https://learn.microsoft.com/api/credentials/share/en-gb/SebastianCzech-4206/75EFE1603D91C01?sharingId=D68643BFE1C92F0D).

The assessment covers hands-on skills in:

* configuring security for AI services in Microsoft Defender for Cloud – Defender CSPM, cloud workload protection plans, and security policies for AI solutions
* configuring model guardrails and controls in Microsoft Foundry – content filters and blocklists
* validating content filters
* configuring security settings for a Microsoft Foundry environment – managing users and roles, setting up connected resources, and configuring diagnostic settings

Applied Skills assessments are based on interactive labs – instead of answering theory questions, you perform real tasks in an Azure environment. That makes them much closer to actual work than a traditional exam.
